Meatloaf

Description

Meatloaf is a dish of ground meat that has been combined with other ingredients and formed into the shape of a loaf, then baked or smoked. The final shape is either hand-formed on a baking tray, or pan-formed by cooking it in a loaf pan.

Ingredients

  • For the loaf:
    1. Ground Meat
    2. An Egg
    3. An onion
    4. Milk
    5. Bread Crumbs
    6. Salt
    7. Pepper
  • For the sauce:
    1. Ketchup
    2. Brown Sugar
    3. Mustard

Steps

  1. Mix the loaf ingredients, then transfer to a loaf pan.
  2. Mix the sauce ingredients, then pour over the loaf.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the meatloaf is no longer pink in the center.
